Massachusetts

Massachusetts is a cornerstone of American history, education, and culture. Located in the New England region, it blends scenic Atlantic coastlines with bustling urban centers like Boston. It is the birthplace of the American Revolution and home to the nation's oldest universities. Massachusetts holds a vital and sacred place in early Latter-day Saint history. Many early Church leaders lived, taught, and built foundations of faith within the state.
